# Shipping Policy
Orders ship within 2 business days after payment.
Standard shipping uses tracked carriers.
Typical delivery time is 3-5 business days.
Expedited delivery is available at checkout.
Returns are handled separately under the refund policy.
If you need a refund, you can get my money back via returns.
Data retention for shipping logs is 90 days.
To delete account data used for shipping, submit a delete account request.
Missing packages must be reported within 7 days of delivery.
