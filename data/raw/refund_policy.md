# Refund Policy
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery.
To request a refund, contact support with your order number.
Eligible refunds are issued to the original payment method.
If you need to get my money back, start a return within the window.
Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the item is defective.
Delivery time does not affect refund eligibility.
Data retention for refund records is 2 years.
To delete account data tied to returns, submit a delete account request.
We may deny returns for used or damaged items.
