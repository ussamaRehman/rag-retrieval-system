# Privacy Policy
We collect account data to process orders and returns.
Data retention: we keep billing records for 2 years.
You can delete account data from your profile settings.
If you want to erase my data, submit a delete account request.
Refund inquiries may reference order history.
Shipping and delivery addresses are stored for troubleshooting.
We use limited data for fraud prevention and refunds.
You may request a copy of your data at any time.
We do not sell personal information.
